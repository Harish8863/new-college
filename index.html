Intermediate

In intermediate class coding,We study programming fundamentals, algorithms, data structures, OOP concepts, basic web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), database basics (SQL), and problem-solving using Python, C++, or Java.

Under Graduate

In undergraduate coding, We study advanced programming, algorithms, data structures, databases, operating systems, software engineering, computer networks, web development, AI/ML basics, cybersecurity, version control, and problem-solving in competitive coding.

In postgraduate coding, We focus on advanced algorithms, specialized domains like AI/ML, big data, blockchain, cloud computing, software architecture, system design, research methodologies, optimization techniques, and industry-focused projects or innovations.

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) branches encompass a wide range of disciplines focusing on computing systems, software development, and technology innovation.,
Specilization

Information Technology
Information Technology (IT) encompasses various branches that focus on the efficient use and management of technology for solving real-world problems. It includes Software Development, which involves creating and maintaining applications for diverse purposes. Web Development focuses on building websites and web applications, ensuring functionality and user-friendly design. Cybersecurity is critical for protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

Electronics And Communication Enginnering
Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) encompasses a wide range of fields focusing on electronic systems, communication technologies, and signal processing. It includes VLSI design, which involves creating microchips and integrated circuits used in advanced electronic devices. Embedded systems play a significant role, combining hardware and software to power devices like IoT systems and smart appliances.
